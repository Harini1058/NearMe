Marina Beach in Chennai is the world's second-longest urban beach, stretching for 13 kilometers along the Bay of Bengal and serving as a cultural hub with historical landmarks. Visitors enjoy relaxing strolls on its sands, watching the sunrise and sunset, and exploring the various statues and memorials that line its promenade. The beach is a bustling spot for both locals and tourists, offering food stalls, recreation, and a lively atmosphere, especially during evenings and festivals.
